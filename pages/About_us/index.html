About VolunKey

VolunKey is a volunteer management platform designed for community advocates, non-profits, and civic leaders who want to organize, manage, and promote volunteer opportunities. The app helps individuals easily discover local and virtual volunteer activities, ensuring a seamless and engaging volunteering experience.


To mobilize communities by seamlessly connecting volunteers with meaningful opportunities

VISION

A world where community service is accessible, inclusive, and impactful.

✔ Community Volunteers – Individuals looking for meaningful opportunities.

✔ Non-Profit Organizations – Need a reliable volunteer management system.

✔ Corporate Social Responsibility Teams – Want to engage employees in volunteering.

✔ Educational Institutions – Seeking a structured way to track student service hours.

✔ Civic Leaders & Local Governments – Mobilizing communities for greater social impact.
